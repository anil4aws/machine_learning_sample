### Karl Pearson's Co-efficient Correlation 

Xj .....Xn =  x̄ = mean 
Yj .....Jn =  ȳ = mean 
Cov(X, Y) = {Σ(Xj-x)(Yj-ȳ)} / n 
Where Cov(X, Y) represents the covariance of variables X and Y.
Summation extends from 1..n
